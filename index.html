Speaking Like an Orc – Tips
  • Never say I — use me (or ee / meeb)
  • Drop proper grammar. Speak broken, blunt, angry.
  • Swap -er endings → 'r  (runner = run'r)
  • Swap -ing endings → 'n  (chopping = chop'n)
  • Spell words how dey sound: "da", "dem", "dat", "der", "dis"
  • Laughter is always Har har! — never LOL/ROFL
  • Orcs barely count past five. Five is h, ten is hh.
  • Unfamiliar words? Just butcher 'em. Dat's Orcish.
