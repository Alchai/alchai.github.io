⚔  Speaking Like an Ork – Rules & Tips
  • Never say I — use Meeb or your orc name
  • Never say you — use Lat (one ork) or Latz (many)
  • Use proper punctuation and capitalise where it makes sense
  • Weapon-wielder suffix: add ur to weapon name  (Lusk → Luskur, Womp → Wompur)
  • Swap -ing → 'n  (chopping = chop'n)  ·  -er → ur
  • Speak broken, blunt, and angry — orcs hate wasting words
  • Party / guild chat is called Zuul Lynk
  • Laughter is always Har har! — never LOL or ROFL
  • Unfamiliar words? Just butcher 'em and move on. Dat's Orcish.
